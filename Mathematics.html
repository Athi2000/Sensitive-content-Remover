Mathematics

Ratio

A comparison of two numbers or quantities. They are measured in the same or similar units. Example: If the ratio of adults to children is 2 to 5, then there are two adults for every 5 children. So, if there are 50 children in attendance, then there are 20 adults.


Rate

A special ratio that compares two quantities measured in different types of units.


Unit rate

A rate with a denominator of 1.


Proportion

An equation of two equivalent ratios.


Equivalent proportions

Proportions that are essentially the same although they look a little different.
